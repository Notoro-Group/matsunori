FAQ

Good questions

DO YOU OFFER TAKEOUT OR DELIVERY?+
We do not currently offer takeout at Fenway — every roll is handed over the counter, seconds after it's rolled.
DO YOU TAKE RESERVATIONS?+
We do not take reservations at this time. Only walk-ins.
WHAT TYPE OF CUISINE DO YOU SPECIALIZE IN?+
We specialize in Temaki Sushi, otherwise known as handrolls. Another way you can think of us is Sushi Tacos!
DO YOU HAVE VEGAN OR VEGETARIAN OPTIONS?+
We have vegetarian friendly options, but nothing fully vegan at this time.
WHAT FORMS OF PAYMENT DO YOU TAKE?+
We accept all credit cards and cash. Including Apple/Google tap to pay.

Not at the moment, possibly in the future.
